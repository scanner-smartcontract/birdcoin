BirdCoin (BIRD)

BirdCoin (BIRD) is a BEP-20 token developed on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC), designed to provide security, transparency, and long-term benefits for its holders.

The token features an auto-burn mechanism to sustain value and a controlled sell limit to prevent market manipulation. With these mechanisms, BirdCoin aims to maintain a balance between liquidity and value growth.
